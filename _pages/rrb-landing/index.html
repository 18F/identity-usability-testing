Login Required

Online services that require a Pin Password (PPW) account. First time users must establish an account by requesting a Password Request Code (PRC) be mailed to their home address.

The following Internet services are intended for railroad employees who are not receiving annuities.

RRB.gov will be down on the first Saturday of the month at 3:00 AM CST for 5 minutes for routine maintenance.

Apply for Unemployment Benefits, Claim Unemployment Benefits and View RUIA Account Statement are unavailable Monday and Thursday, from 11:00 PM to Midnight CST for routine maintenance.

All Internet Services that require login will experience sporadic service Friday 10:30 PM to Saturday 5:00 AM CST for routine maintenance.
