Account reset

Resetting your account is an irreversible action, so making sure this is the action you want to take is very important. Please review the information below before continuing.

Step 1: Don’t have your personal Key?

Your personal key is a 12 character/number code that was given to you at account creation as a recovery method. You were told to either print it out or write it down somewhere—see example below.

If you found your personal key, you can use it to access your account.

Step 2: Account reset
